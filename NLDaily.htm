Milwaukee Braves in 12 innings by the score of 5 to 3 at County
Stadium.
 
Both teams had scored 3 runs after nine full innings.  Cincinnati won the game
in the 12th inning.  Willie Jones opened up the inning nicely when he drew a
walk.  Jim Bailey came next and he laid down a sacrifice bunt.  One out
later, Temple then lashed a single plating two runs.  Temple took second on
the throw.  Cincinnati totaled 10 hits on the afternoon.  
 
The winning pitcher was Bailey(1-0) who allowed no runs in 3 and 1/3 innings.
Bailey was helped out by Orlando Pena who recorded his 1st save.  Joey
Jay(0-3) was the loser in relief.  He surrendered 1 hit and 2 walks in 2 and
1/3 innings.
