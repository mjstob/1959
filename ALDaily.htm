complete game and center fielder Willie Tasby had 4 hits and 2 RBI as the
Baltimore Orioles defeated the Kansas City Athletics by the score of 10 to 2
at Kansas City.
 
Tasby was a seemingly constant irritant to the hometown fans as he provided
much of the offense for the visitors.  He singled in the 1st inning, slapped
a single in the 4th inning, smashed a two-run homer in the 6th inning and
doubled in the 9th inning.  Offensively, Baltimore was a juggernaut.  They
scored in 6 separate innings.

was hit with the loss.  He surrended 3 homeruns in his 9 innings.
