Camilo Pascual carried a no-hitter into the 7th inning at Griffith Stadium as
the Washington Senators beat the Boston Red Sox 2 to 0.
 
Pascual(1-1) was tremendous on the mound for Washington.  He gave up just 1
hit and 1 walk in 9 innings dropping his ERA from 4.50 to 2.74.  The
right-handed Pascual had his no-hit bid broken up by Frank Malzone who
singled with one out in the 7th inning.  Neither team managed to score after
the 4th inning.  
 
The loss was charged to Bill Monbouquette(0-2).  Pascual was obviously very
pleased with his performance in the aftermath of the game, 'It was just one
of those games where everything worked.  My arm felt great, heck, I could
have pitched another two or three innings.  I only hope that I can do it
again next time out!'
