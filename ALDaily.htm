Senators beat the Boston Red Sox by the score of 12 to 10.

in the first inning scoring a single run on 3 base hits.  Washington rapped
out 16 hits for the afternoon.

best stuff,' Cookie Lavagetto said after the game.  'Kemmerer did not have
command of his pitches most of the afternoon, but he proved he really knew
how to pitch!'
