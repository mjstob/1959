runs on 4 hits.  The critical plate appearance was by Lemon who deflated the

out-hit by Washington, 13 hits to 10.

runs. Mike Fornieles(6-7) was the losing pitcher in relief.  He was knocked
around, surrendering 4 runs in 2 innings.
